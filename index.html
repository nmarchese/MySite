About

Hi, I'm Nik! I am a full-stack software engineer with experience in a wide variety of in demand technologies. I am passionate about problem solving, which is what drives me to learn and grow as I move forward in my career. In my three years as a professional Java developer I worked on an enterprise level application which utilized a diverse tech stack to solve complex problems. This experience allowed me to refine my skills at writing quality code that is both simple and understandable while also being scalable and fully testable. In addition to my responsibilities as a developer I have also held the position of Scrum Master on an Agile team within an organization practicing Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) methodologies.

I am at my best when I am part of an adaptable and collaborative team where team members both learn from as well as teach each other in order to grow and thrive. I am hard working and a very fast learner; I obtained the Oracle Java SE8 Programmer 1 certification less than two months after writing my first line of code. Learning new technologies and broadening my understanding of software are ever ongoing goals for me. Most recently I have been Learning React and will be working on learning Python soon, in addition to continuing to work with Java and pushing my knowledge of AWS.

Currently, I am between software jobs while living abroad in Australia. I will be returning to the United States eventually, although when that will be is still up in the air. While abroad I am lucky enough to be living in a place that is significantly better off than many others around the world during this ever evolving COVID-19 pandemic. Taking advantage of that fact, I spend most of my time split between personal software projects and enjoying life outdoors in northeast Australia (hiking, running, lots of trips to the beach, and even trying to teach myself to surf!).

On a professional level, right now I am looking to continue my career in a remote position or in a position on site here in Australia.
